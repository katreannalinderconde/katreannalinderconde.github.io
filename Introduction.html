Introducing Me

My name is Katreanna Arabela Linder-Conde. I was born in Miami, Florida on June 23rd, 2004. Growing up with just my mom and grandparents has definitely made my life different, but I'm grateful for the opputunity to be surrounded by people I love. Living in a city infused with culture has shown me new ways to do things, and it's made my world a colorful place. Despite what many people think, however, I'm not just from the U.S. My mom and grandfather were actually born in Caracas, Venezuela, and my grandma was born in Guatemala City, Guatemala. Further back, I'm also German and Dutch.

MORE

Photography

Something I'd like to do in the future is to do something I Iove, take pictures, and sell them for money. Photography is about expressing yourself, and this is something I see myself enjoying.

High School Job

I think getting an after school job is an important milestone in someone's life. I'd like to get a high school job so I can have income coming in not only for myself but also to help my family. This job could help me be more responsible and teach me the value of money, something that could help me in the future.

Design

One of my future career choices invoves designing. I love fashion, and since a young age I've wanted to make my own clothes. It's another way of expressing myself, and it's a career in which I can make creative decisions without being judged for who I am.

Animal Care

Since before I was born, I've been surrounded by animals. I have 3 dogs at home, and I ride horses somewhere where cats are practically on you. I see the animals without a loving home, and I know I'd like to help. So, hopefully after becoming a successful adult, I'd like to open an animal shelter.

World Traveler

Traveling is huge for me. It allows me to see things I've never seen before and explore different cultures and values. I usually stay within the country, but I've also been to Paris, France and Caracas, Venezuela. These beautiful places allow me to make memories with the people I love. So, traveling the world at some point would be an amazing opportunity.
